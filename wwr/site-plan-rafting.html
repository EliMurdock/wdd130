Overview

Purpose

The purpose this site will serve is to share information and opinions about rafting and other outdoor activities. It will not be a comprehensive list, but rather a quick and easy list, mostly useable by those who would know me and take my reccomendations.

Audience

My target audience is primarily my friends, although classmates, teachers, and family are also welcome to view. These people need information primarily for amusement, but they may find some of the things on the website useful if they are planning on going river rafting or another rexburg outdoor activity anytime soon. Thier river rafting needs are not currently being met. This site will primarily be accessed on desktop by those planning or interested in outdoor activities
