Welcome to Quick DND!

This site is designed to help you through the difficult choices that come during character creation! The three basics of character creation, as well as the most important choices you'll make during it, are which race, class, and background your character will have. To help with that, I've compiled the resources below to serve as an easy reference when comparing the different options you have. To start, scroll down and view the races below. Write your choice of race on your character sheet before progressing to the next page. Do the same for each page and voila! You now have a character framework, ready to go for your next DND session.
